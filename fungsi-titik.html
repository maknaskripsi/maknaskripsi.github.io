[tex]{\boxed{\ \: \tt y = a(x - xp)² + yp\:\:\:}}

Titik puncak (-4,-15)

xp = -4yp = -15

Titik lain (-2, -13)

x = -2y = -13

Masukkan ke rumus untuk mencari nilai a.

y = a(x - xp)² + yp

-13 = a(-2 - (-4))² - 15

-13 = a(2)² - 15

-13 = 4a - 15

-13 + 15 = 4a

2 = 4a

2/4 = a

1/2 = a

Masukkan nilai a ke rumus, tapi x dan y tidak di ubah.

y = a(x - xp)² + yp

= 1/2 (x - (-4)² - 15

= 1/2 (x + 4)² - 15

= 1/2 ((x + 4)(x + 4)) - 15

= 1/2 (x² + 4x + 4x + 16) - 15

= 1/2 (x² + 8x + 16) - 15

= 1/2x² + 4x + 8 - 15

= 1/2x² + 4x - 7

Jadi, f(x) = 1/2x² + 4x - 7
