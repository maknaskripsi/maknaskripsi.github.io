5. prosedur text jasmine ice tea​


Jawaban:

How to Make Jasmine Iced Tea

Ingredients

10 1/2 cups water5 jasmine tea bags1 cup sugarIce cubes

Instructions Checklist

Step 1

Bring 10 cups of water to a boil in a large saucepan. Place jasmine tea bags in a large heatproof container. Add boiling water, and steep for 3 minutes. Remove tea bags. Let cool completely.

Step 2

Meanwhile, bring sugar and remaining 1/2 cup water to a boil in a small saucepan, stirring, until sugar dissolves. Let cool completely.

Step 3

Stir together tea and 3/4 cup simple syrup, plus more if needed, depending on desired sweetness. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour. Divide among ice-filled glasses.

21. Tuliskan kedalam Bahasa Inggris How to make Ice Tea


Ingredients:

3-4 black tea bags

Sugar to taste

Ice cubes

Water

Mint (optional)


1 . Bring 2 cups (480 ml) of water to a rapid boil. You can boil the water in a s          small pan or a pot.

2. Turn off the heat.

3. Add 3-5 bags of your favorite black tea. Ceylon and Keemun teas can work best because they won't turn cloudy when they're left to stand. You can even choose a special tea blend that is made specifically for iced tea.

4.  Leave the teabags in the hot water for 5 minutes. Any longer than that and the tea will be too bitter. If you leave them in for less time than that, then the tea will be too weak. This will be a very strong mixture of tea -- you'll dilute it with water momentarily. Remove the tea bags after the time has passed.

5.  Pour the tea into a pitcher. Wait 5-10 minutes for it to cool down.

6. Pour 2 cups (480 ml) of cold water into the tea. This will dilute the tea and will make the mixture less strong. You can stir the mixture for best results.

7. Refrigerate the mixture until it's chilled. This should take 2-3 hours.

8. Serve the tea. Pour the tea into a tall glass that is filled with ice. Squeeze a slice of lemon into the tea and add a sprig of mint to the top. If you'd like to add sugar, start by stirring in half a teaspoon and add more to taste.

= dini drinks ice tea everyday
