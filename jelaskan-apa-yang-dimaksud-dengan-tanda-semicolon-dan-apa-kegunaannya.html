Penggunaan simbol semicolon (;) pada program bahasa C++ adalah untuk memberi tau komputer bahwa garis perintah pada pemrograman sudah mencapai titik akhir perintahnya.

Thesis: it presents announcement of issue of concern.Arguments: it presents the reasons for concern, leading to recommendation.Recommendation: it presents the statement of what ought or ought not to happen.

The purpose of hortatory exposition is to persuade the reader or listener that something should or should not be the case.

12. Joining sentences with Conjunction Adverbs (Compound Sentenses) Combine earch group of sentences wtih one the conjunctive adverb. (Moreover, In Addition, Besides, Otherwise) Use semicolon to replace the period of the frist sentence ; use a comma to cut off the conjunctive adverb. NOTE which of the three adverbial positions the conjunctive adverb mau occupy. 1. Mr. Kenneth is an intelligent and stimulating teacher. Also, he takes an interest in the personal well-being of his students. 2. John is a very lazy student. Also, he always comes late to Class. 3. It's too late to go to the movies Also, i don't like the picture they're playing. 4. I don't feel like going to that party. Also, i have a bad cold, so i shouldn't go out 5. We must all eat the proper food. If we don't, we'll get sick 6. He didn't study hard enough If he had, he would have passed the examination 7. Keep of my property If you don't, I'll have you arrested.


Jawaban

According to the instructions above to use a semicolon after the first sentence and adding the suitable conjunction adverbs before the second sentence, below is the complete joined sentences:

Mr. Kenneth is an intelligent and stimulating teacher; in addition, he takes an interest in the personal well-being of his students.John is a very lazy student; moreover, he always comes late to class.It's too late to go to the movies; besides, I don't like the picture they're playing.I don't feel like going to that party; moreover, I have a bad cold, so I shouldn't go out.We must all eat the proper food; otherwise, we'll get sick.He didn't study hard enough; otherwise, he would have passed the examination.Keep of my property; otherwise, I'll have you arrested.

Pembahasan

Conjunction adverbs digunakan untuk menggabungkan dua klausa menjadi satu kalimat. Dalam hal ini klausa kedua akan menjadi keterangan dari klausa sebelumnya. Dalam Bahasa Inggris untuk menyambungkan dua klausa dapat menggunakan kata penghubung in addition, moreover, besides, dan otherwise. Moreover dan in addition digunakan untuk menambahkan informasi atau mendukung pernyataan sebelumnya. Besides memiliki makna yang sama dengan moreover dan in addition namun lebih informal.
