2. Membuat kalimat Cause and Effect sentences dari kalimat tersebut

His town is the cleanest town in the province so it got the Adipura Award. The people did not care so the town was dirty and messy. The government and the people have been working hard together so it is clean and beautiful now. There were not many trees so it was hot and dry. Three years ago the mayor launched a program to clean the town and to plant trees so it is clean and green now. The government planned the program together with them so the people in town are happy to take part in it. Mr. Satrio started smoking a long time ago when he was in SMP so when he is trying to stop smoking, he says it is not easy. He often has bad coughs and gets sick easily so he believes 100% that smoking is not good for his health. He knows it is not easy to cut down cigarettes so he keeps trying.Vina is the eldest of four children in her family so she does the chores and takes care of her younger sisters and brother all the time.She can manage her time very well so she can always finish her homework, and she always gets good marks.  Pembahasan

Bagian kalimat yang dicetak tebal adalah Cause atau sebab dan bagian kalimat yang dicetak miring adalah Effect atau akibat. Kata penghubung yang digunakan adalah so yang berarti sehingga. Hal ini menyatakan, bagian kalimat setelah kata hubung so merupakan akibat dari kegiatan atau aktivitas atau hal yang diungkapkan sebelumnya. Contohnya pada kalimat pertama:

His town is the cleanest town in the province so it got the Adipura Award.

Artinya: Kota tempat tinggalnya adalah kota paling bersih di provinsi itu sehingga kota tersebut memperoleh penghargaan Adipura.

Kalimat di atas merupakan contoh complex sentence. It consists of 1 dependent clause and 1 independent clause.

25. Cause : Because of bad weather Effect : Cause : Owing to bad weather Effect :


Cause and Effect

Cause : Because of bad weather

Effect :  all flights are cancelled

=> Because of bad weather, all flights are cancelled

(Karena cuaca buruk, semua penerbangan dibatalkan)

Cause : Owing to bad weather  

Effect :  the crash is happened

=> Owing to bad weather, the crash is happened

(Karena cuaca buruk, kecelakaan itu terjadi)

PEMBAHASAN:

Pada dasarnya antara because of dan owing to memiliki makna yang sama dan sama- sama preposition yang mana bisa dimaknai sebagai kata penghubung pula, yang menjelaskan alasan atau mengungkapkan ‘karena apa’ sesuatu terjadi  dan diikuti oleh kata benda, bukan klausa.

Cause and Effect

Cause adalah:

The reason or motive, for an action Why something happen Happen first

Effect adalah:

Result or conclusion of an action What happened Happens due to a cause

Jadi cause and effect biasa juga disebut dengan kalimat sebab-akibat atau kalimat kausalitas adalah kalimat yang dibangun dengan melibatkan tindakan yang membuat sesuatu terjadi dan hasil dari tindakan tersebut. Paling gampang ditandai dengan kata hubung yang menandakan sebab akibat.

Signal words of cause

Ketika kita bicara tentang cause maka kata- kata yang dapat kita gunakan adalah sebagai berikut:

Because  the reason for  On account of  Bring about  Give rise to f Created by  Contributed to  Led to  Due to  For this reason  Unless

Signal words of Effect

Ketika kita bicara tentang effect maka kata- kata yang dapat kita gunakan adalah sebagai berikut:

As a result  Then  Hence  For this reason Outcome  So  Consequently  Finally  Therefore  In order to

The sentence structure of cause and effect

Due to/Because of/owing to/Thanks to + noun Because/Since/As/For/As a result of + subject + verb

Contoh:

Owing to her hard work and intelligence, we won the trophy.  (Berkat kerja keras dan kecerdasannya, kami memenangkan trofi.)Thanks to Ammar and Ameera’s effective planning, the event went well. ( Berkat perencanaan efektif Ammar dan Ameera, acara ini berjalan dengan baik)Because of his hard work, he managed to get the best student award . (Karena kerja kerasnya, ia berhasil mendapatkan penghargaan siswa terbaik)I have  a stomach ache because I ate too much food . (Perut saya sakit karena makan terlalu banyak)There was a lot of homework and test, as a result most of the students were unhappy and couldn’t go anywhere during the weekend.  (Ada banyak pekerjaan rumah dan ujian, akibatnya sebagian besar siswa tidak bahagia dan tidak bisa pergi ke mana pun selama akhir pekan.)Since Iqbal has been promoted as a manager so he always does his best in everything (Karena Iqbal telah dipromosikan sebagai manajer, maka ia selalu melakukan yang terbaik dalam segala hal)

Note : Hal yang perlu diperhatikan dalam membentuk kalimat cause and effect  adalah jika kata hubung terletak di awal kalimat, maka kita perlu menggunakan tanda baca koma. Namun, jika kata hubungnya berada di tengah, kita tidak perlu menggunakan koma.

29. Can nuclear weapons cause earthquakes? Can landslide cause earthquakes? Can vulcanic eruptions cause an earthquakes?


Jawaban:

1.Nuclear explosions can induce small earthquakes along existing faults near a test site. Some underground nuclear tests have fractured the ground surface above the explosions, causing movement on faults adjacent to explosion sites.

2.In areas affected by Typhoon Morakot–related landslides, the researchers found a threefold increase in the earthquake rate immediately following the storm, they report today in Scientific Reports . They also found an uptick in the proportion of small earthquakes after the storm.

3.Volcanically-caused long period earthquakes are produced by vibrations generated by the movement of magma or other fluids within the volcano. Pressure within the system increases and the surrounding rock fails, creating small earthquakes.

Maaf kalau salah

1. Yes, because nuclear causes surface waves that cause earthquakes

2. Yes, landslides can cause earthquakes but the impact is small and the area is narrow

36. rumus dari cause dan effect​


Jawaban:

A. Kalimat cause-effect yang diikuti oleh noun

Kata sebab akibat yang diikuti dengan noun atau noun phrase biasanya menggunakan kata atau frasa (signal word/phase) seperti :

Because of

Thanks to

As a result of

Due to

As a consequence of

Owing to

Formula :

Signal word/phrase + Cause + Effect

Contoh :

Due to Bella’s nervousness, she did not pass the driving test.

Signal phrase: due to

Cause: Bella’s nervousness

Effect: She did not pass the driving test

B. Kalimat sebab-akibat yang diikuti oleh verb

Kata sebab akibat yang diikuti dengan subjek + verb atau verb phrase biasanya menggunakan kata atau frasa (signal word/phase) seperti :

Because

Since

As

For

Formula:

Signal word/phrase + Cause + Effect

Contoh :

Because Bella was nervous, she did not pass the driving test

Signal phrase: because

Cause: Bella was nervous

Effect: She did not pass the driving test

-Clause adalah kelompok kata yang mengandung subject dan predicate. Clause mungkin berupa sentence (dapat berdiri sendiri sebagai suatu kalimat: independent clause). Ciri-ciri Clause

Adapun ciri-ciri klausa adalah sebagai berikut:

1) Dalam klausa terdapat satu predikat, tidak lebih dan tidak kurang;

2) Klausa dapat menjadi kalimat jika kepadanya dikenai intonasi final;

3) Dalam kalimat plural, klausa merupakan bagian dari kalimat;

4) Klausa dapat diperluas dengan menambahkan atribut fungsi-fungsi yang belum terdapat dalam klausa tersebut; selain dengan penambahan konstituen atribut pada salah satu atau setiap fungsi sintaktis yang ada.
