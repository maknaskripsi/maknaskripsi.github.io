The most sophisticated and delicious dish in the world, which is internationally acknowledged, has its ORIGIN in Padang, West Sumatra. Do you know what that is?

Choosing the appropriate word to fill the blanks is not too difficult if we've known the meaning of each given words.

Based on the text, it is talking about a famous food that is internationally acknowledged as the most sophisticated and delicious dish in the world.

Well, let's see the place of the blanks.

The most sophisticated and delicious dish in the world, which is internationally acknowledged, has its _______ in Padang, West Sumatra. Do you know what that is?

The blanks is put after a possessive pronoun "its" and before an adverb of place "in Padang". It means, the blanks must be filled by a noun that has a connection to the adverb. So the most suitable word to fill the blanks is ORIGIN, and the text will be : The most sophisticated and delicious dish in the world, which is internationally acknowledged, has its ORIGIN in Padang, West Sumatra. Do you know what that is?
