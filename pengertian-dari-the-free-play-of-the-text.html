8. Write the definition of play and write some of the famous playwright


The parable of play:
If you take part in a game or a sport, you play it. You might love to play charades, but no one is going to want to play with you if you keep forgetting the rules. The verb play also means to make music, like when you play your ukulele. And if you toy with a concept — like forming a ukulele orchestra — you play around with the idea.


Answer:
The definition of Play are:
"Engage in recreational activities rather than work; occupy oneself in a diversion."
or
"Be at play; be engaged in playful activity; amuse oneself in a way characteristic of children."

E.g:
1. I used to play with trucks as a little girl.
2. Last spring, he did not play until mid-March.

28. We play volleyball. The passive voice of the sentence is?


“We play volleyball”. The passive voice of the sentence is?

Jawaban:

Volleyball is played by us ✅

Structure kalimat:

We = subject jamak

play = verb 1

volleyball = object

Berdasarkan Structure kalimat di atas bisa dipastikan bahwa kalimat tersebut adalah Present Tense bentuk Active:

(+) S + VERB 1 (-s/es) + O

= We + play + volleyball.

(Kami + bermain + bola voli)

Maka kalimat Passive:

(+) S + TO BE (is/am/are) + VERB 3 + By

= Volleyball + is + played + by us

(Bola voli + dimainkan + oleh kami)
